ICO Mafia – House Rules

Solicitation - Do not send vendor solicitation of any kind to the group or privately to group members. You may share about your product or solution in the group, but don't promote any product or ICO. If anyone has an interest/query, they will reach out to you.

Confidentiality - Everything in this group is off the record. Whatever happens or is discussed here, stays here. This is an invite only group and no media or regulators are allowed.

Decorum - Keep the conversation civil and don’t get into personal attacks or extended debates. Treat this chat group as you would an in-person group meeting. If you find yourself in an extended debate, consider taking it to a private chat. Be considerate that others may not be interested in your conversation.

Referrals - If you think there is someone who should be part of this group, send a private message to an admin and we will evaluate it on a case by case basis.

Recruitment - If you would like to hire people for your own team, you may post a requirement but NO professional recruiters or HR managers are allowed in the group.

Illegal Activity – If any group member is suspected of being involved in engaging in illegal activity, they will be removed immediately.

Off-Topic Conversations – If you find yourself in a chatty dialogue with another person, please take it private as this group is growing and others may not be interested in following.

Lurkers – This is a community based on exchange. If you are not contributing (and we know who you are) we will delete the bottom contributors from time to time.

Removal - Breach of any of the above rules shall lead to immediate removal from the group at the full discretion of the admins.

IF you have any questions or comments, please reach out to an admin.
