Stablecoins: designing a price-stable cryptocurrency

A useful currency should be a medium of exchange, a unit of account, and a store of value. Cryptocurrencies excel at the first, but as a store of value or unit of account, they're pretty bad. You cannot be an effective store of value if your price fluctuates by 20% on a normal day.

Digital Currency and Blockchain: How are Financial Institutions Responding?

Perhaps the industry most obviously threatened by blockchain is banking and financial services. Digital currencies and blockchain platforms are by nature in the business of putting financial overlords out of business. If individuals and institutions can transact directly at a significantly lower cost due to the removal of exchange middlemen, then there is less of a need for traditional banks and other controllers of the money game.
