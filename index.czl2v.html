Crypto-Mania Grips Hong Kong as City Looks Beyond Banking

China blocks WhatsApp

China has blocked WhatsApp, security experts confirmed today . Over the past few months, WhatsApp has experienced brief disruptions to service, with users unable to send video chats or photos. Now, even text messages are completely blocked, according to Nadim Kobeissi, an applied cryptographer at Symbolic Software, a Paris-based research firm that also monitors digital censorship in China.
